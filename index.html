About Me

Hi, I’m Harsh Desai, the founder of VM Studio, based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. I’m a passionate and professional Graphic Designer and Motion Video Editor with over 1.3 years of freelance experience.

Currently, I’m pursuing my Bachelor of Computer Applications (2023–2026), which helps me combine technical knowledge with creative skills to deliver high-quality visual content.

Through my own brand, VM Studio, I have worked with a variety of clients, creating eye-catching posters, social media designs, and engaging video content. My work focuses on delivering visually appealing designs that help brands stand out and connect with their audience.

In addition to freelancing, I am also working remotely with a private company, Universal Property, where I specialize in video editing and reel cover design. This experience has helped me improve my consistency, creativity, and understanding of client requirements in a professional work environment.

I am skilled in tools like Adobe Photoshop, After Effects, Premiere Pro, and CapCut, which allow me to create high-quality designs and motion visuals.

My goal is to deliver creative, impactful, and high-quality work that helps brands grow, attract attention, and leave a strong impression.
